# Methodology Variations Across Engagements

## Full-Spectrum Assessment (AI Threat Exposure Review)

- **Coverage**: All 4 trust boundaries tested
- **Depth**: Breadth-first, 3-5 issues per boundary
- **Duration**: 2-3 weeks
- **Best for**: Pre-deployment validation, compliance, M&A due diligence

## Focused Deep Dive (e.g., LLM App Red Team)

- **Coverage**: 1-2 trust boundaries
- **Depth**: Deep testing of 8-12 issues with exhaustive technique variations
- **Duration**: 1-2 weeks
- **Best for**: Known risk area, post-incident investigation, specialized threats

## Continuous Validation (Monitoring Setup)

- **Coverage**: Selected high-risk issues as continuous regression tests
- **Depth**: Automated probes with alerting
- **Duration**: 3 days setup + ongoing
- **Best for**: Production systems requiring continuous assurance

## Purple Team Collaboration (Workshop)

- **Coverage**: Customer-defined priority areas
- **Depth**: Live demonstration + defensive capability development
- **Duration**: 2 days
- **Best for**: Internal team training, SIEM rule development, incident response prep
